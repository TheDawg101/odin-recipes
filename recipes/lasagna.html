Lasagna

The Perfect Home Cooked Lasagna!

This tasty looking lasagna is an easy to make dish, consisting of lasagna pasta, ground beef, marinara sauce, lots of veggies, and of course cheese!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Beef
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Carrots
  • Peppers
  • Tomatoes
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Mix the Ground Beef, Sauce, Vegitables into a bowl
  2. Layer the mix on the bottom of a buttered glass pan
  3. Layer with a cover of pasta noodles
  4. Layer with Cheese
  5. Repeat steps 2,3,4 as often as you like
  6. Cook for 10 hours at 200 F
  7. Let cool and enjoy!
